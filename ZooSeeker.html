ZooSeeker is an Android application built for patrons visting the San Diego Zoo. The Zoo houses 4000 animals of more than 650 species and subspecies on a property of 100 acres and brings in around 4 million visitors annually. Navigating the zoo can be quite the hassel for new visitors.

ZooSeeker allows visitors to select the exhibits they wish to see and plans out the optimal exhibit visitation route with directions. The app tracks user location and allows users to replan their route if they find themselves off track. ZooSeeker makes navigating the San Deigo Zoo effortless.

My Role
  • Used Agile principles to collaborate with team of 6 to meet client expectations and deliver the product in a timely manner.
  • Implemented location tracking, location mocking and pathfinding algorithm using appropriate design patterns in Java.
  • Devised tests using JUnit and Espresso to test the functionality and interactivity of the application.
